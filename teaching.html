Teaching and Outreach

I am currently the AMOS Education and Outreach Committee Chair, coordinating Australia-wide weather, climate and ocean outreach and educational programs. I was an instructor on board the RV Investigator for the CAPSTAN program, sailing from Hobart to Fremantle in April 2019, and will be returning as Chief Scientist in March 2020. I previously developed curriculum and taught a high school summer class on environmental science and policy, wrote for Oceanbites, and kept a cruise blog on a trip to the Southern Ocean
