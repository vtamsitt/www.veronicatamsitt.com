Research

I am interested in understanding the influence of the Southern Ocean on global climate, using ship-based observations, autonomous research platforms and geophysical models. I am currently working with Professor Lynne Talley at Scripps Institution of Oceanography, using the Southern Ocean State Estimate (SOSE) to understand spatial variations in Southern Ocean heat budgets and upwelling, in collaboration with the Southern Ocean Carbon and Climate Observations and Modeling (SOCCOM) project. I am particularly interested in:

  • global ocean circulation
  • Southern Ocean dynamics
  • air-sea fluxes
  • ice-ocean interactions
  • physical controls on biogeochemistry
